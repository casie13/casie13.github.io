Approach

I work where the stakes are real, and that changes how research gets done.

Sensitive data, regulated environments, and people in vulnerable positions don't tolerate sloppy method. My background is mixed-methods: interviews and qualitative analysis sit alongside survey design and quantitative work, and I'm comfortable moving between them depending on what the question needs.

The output that matters isn't a report — it's a decision a team makes differently. Every study below is framed around what changed because the research existed.
